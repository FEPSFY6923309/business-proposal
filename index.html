what services do we offer?

Within the UK thousands of people are killed due a lack of ability of reaching emergency services. Pinq looks to lower this number significantly by 
offering a direst line to emergency services or stored contacts when requested by the user or is unable to. This will be a cheaper alternative to
buying a fitness watch or smart watch which are typically charged for around 50 pounds. Pinq looks to provide a minimalistic yet highly customisable
product for our patrons as well as allowing them to fine tune their desired device. Only neccesary functions will be implanted to Pinq products
including a GPS tracker, green LED to measure the wearer's heart rate and a small amount of storage to store emergency service lines as well as some 
key personal contacts in order to keep our product as affordabal as possible for our customers.

Pinq will also run a free application named after the after the company which will include some other miscellaneous features some of which include a 
live tracker displaying the wearer's current location; displays the wearer's heart rate over the day; allows the user to post a general review in an
area (such as evident dangers in the area) as well as a velocity tracker using the GPS data.

Why did we create Pinq

After much research we as a company have identified how multiple people have been endangered in public due to the lack of ability to call for emergency 
services in dire situations resulting in the victim getting assulted and in more serious situations fatal injury. According to the NHS there were 779
fatalities due to a delay in emergency services as well as an interview conducted by the office of students which revealed that around 25% of female 
students were harrased around campus/dormitories during the night due to the lack contact to emergency services(OFS 2025). If emergency services were 
able to easily get to the victims location quickly then a multitude of casualties would be prevented, leading to the creation of Pinq. Our service
allows the client to have a subtle accessory which will be able to contact local authorities and personal contacts tracking the wearer's position every
thirty seconds storing the location onto the clients mobile device.
